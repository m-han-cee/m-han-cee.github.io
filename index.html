Minghao Han

Chemical and Environmental Engineering Ph.D. Candidate

Hello, I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Chemical and Environmental Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, working with Prof. Don R. Collins on the atmospheric aerosol science and measurement technology. My research integrates laboratory experiments, field campaigns, and instrument development to study the formation, transformation, and impacts of airborne particles. I am passionate about advancing experimental techniques and analytical methods to improve our understanding of air pollution and climate-relevant processes. Welcome to contact me!
